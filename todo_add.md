Liv Café’ & Bistro
It’s always the little things, isn’t it? The coffee just so. The light in the window. That meal that satiates and yet, leaves you wanting more. That’s what we are about here at Liv Café and Bistro. A cocoon to live a little. As often as possible. Nestled within Oak Bay, Victoria’s quaint neighbourhood, Liv is an ingredient-driven restaurant, founded by Chefs Louise Lu and Milton Rebello. Both Louise and Milton have earned his stripes in kitchens across the globe, and the cuisine is a thoughtful coming together of their vast experience.

The philosophy is simple…as the seasons change, so does the menu. Back where we are from, ancient wisdom dictates that we eat according to the seasons, like nature intended. It’s great for you and the environment. Our menu aims to reflect what grows seasonally on our island and around. A creative blend of tradition and innovation, it will change seasonally, pairing global influences with ethically sourced and sustainable local ingredients holding centre stage. Most of the produce is truly homegrown from our own little farm in Sannich, ‘Liv’s Edible Gardens’. At Liv, we focus on simplicity and elegance. We are about creating an experience, not just a meal. Come, Liv a little with us.
Food
We have a farmland in Saanich that we have dedicated to grow fresh ingredients and florals that we offer at the restaurant. We challenge ourselves daily to make Liv Café and Bistro sustainable — we try our best to reduce waste and to make changes to our menu to reflect what is happening at the farms and Ocean around us.
